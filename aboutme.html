My Interests

Diver Down

I'm a certified Advanced SCUBA Diver. My favorite dive has been off the coast of Oahu, Hawai'i when I went free diving with Galapagos and Tiger sharks (no cage). I also went diving through the lava tubes along the Northern sea shore. I am pictured above at my first wreck dive on the Kittiwake in Grand Cayman. My deepest dive was on the Thunderbolt shipwreck in the Florida Keys at 120ft. I hope to continue diving throughout my whole life as my grandfather and father have.

Wondering Woman

Since I was a child I have often traveled with my family and friends. I have been to 5 countries: Ireland, Scotland, Wales, England, and Canada. I have been to nearly half of the states in the USA. I often visit my second home in the Florida Keys and I hope to retire there one day. I plan to visit all of the US National Parks in my life time. So far I've made it to 17/63 parks!

Audacious Adventurer

I very often like to go on adventures with friends or on my own. While I love anything aquatic I try to have fun above sea level. One of my favorite forms of adventure is going on a hike. I recently hiked the Grand Canyon Rim to Rim in one day. It took me 15 hours to complete the 24.5 mile hike! The picture above is me at the very start of my hike. My hope is to eventually retire early and travel for the remainder of my life like my granparents did.
