University Values

John Carroll University draws inspiration from Saint Ignatius of Loyola's experience and the Society of Jesus' centuries-long commitment to academic achievement and devotion to the common good. The University's Jesuit Catholic identity is a single reality founded on the merging of faith and culture. It symbolizes a dedication to a world church that serves humanity's pursuit for truth, value, justice, and solidarity. It also symbolizes adoration for Christ's lofty vision, which he preached and lived as the ultimate manifestation of human fulfillment. John Carroll University, as a Jesuit Catholic university, encourages students to succeed in learning, leadership, and service in the region and beyond the world.
