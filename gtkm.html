My Intrests

Book Worm

I enjoy my quiet reading time when I'm not working on side projects. Barnes and Noble's trips are always fun but I truly enjoy finding local quaint book stores. Sci-fi/ fantasy or biographies of favorite celebrities are my favorite. I love new book recommendations and trading books with friends. I have recently joined a book club with my mother at our local library.

Gym Rat

I have taken pride in my health and I want to be the strongest I can be. Mental and physical health are equally important and I take steps to make both better day to day. I especially love taking moments to myself or communicating with loved ones for peace of mind. I am an avid gym-goer and set monthly goals for myself to keep my motivation high.


My Hobbies

Diver Down

I'm a certified Advanced SCUBA Diver. My favorite dive has been off the coast of Oahu, Hawai'i when I went free diving with Galapagos and Tiger sharks (no cage). I also went diving through the lava tubes along the northern sea shore. The picture to the left was my first wreck dive on the Kittiwake in Grand Cayman. My deepest dive was on the Thunderbolt shipwreck in the Florida Keys at 120ft. I hope to continue diving throughout my whole life as my grandfather and father have.

Wondering Woman

Since I was a child I have often traveled with my family and friends. I have been to 5 countries: Ireland, Scotland, Wales, England, and Canada. I have been to nearly half of the states in the USA. I often visit my second home in the Florida Keys and I hope to retire there one day. Until then, as I work remotely, I hope to move around the country and possibly travel to other countries for work.

Audacious Adventurer

I very often like to go on adventures with friends or on my own. While I love anything aquatic I try to have fun above sea level. One of my favorite forms of adventure is going on a hike. Pictured to the left, I conquered the risky Koko Crater Railway Trailhead and the view lived up to its hype. In the future, I hope to go skydiving, bungee jumping and find more thrill-seeking exploits.
